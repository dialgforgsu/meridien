About the Practice

Committed to the health & beauty of your skin

Meridien Dermatology is dedicated to enhancing the health and appearance of your skin through expert care and the latest dermatological advancements. We provide comprehensive, personalized skincare solutions for every patient.

Led by Dr. So Yeon Paek, our practice serves Dallas and the surrounding areas with a focus on quality, compassion, and clinical excellence — from routine dermatology to complex medical conditions including hidradenitis suppurativa, psoriasis, and skin cancer.

What We Treat

Comprehensive dermatology care

Schedule a Visit

General Dermatology

Acne, eczema, rosacea, psoriasis, vitiligo, hair loss, warts, fungal infections, and comprehensive routine skin care for all ages.

Pediatric Dermatology

Specialized skin care for children and adolescents, including eczema, warts, molluscum, birthmarks, and acne, with a gentle child-friendly approach.

Skin Cancer

Detection and treatment of melanoma, basal cell, and squamous cell carcinoma through thorough full-body skin exams and expert surgical care.

Psoriasis & Atopic Dermatitis

Evidence-based treatment for chronic inflammatory skin conditions, including topical therapies, phototherapy, and advanced biologics.

Biologic Therapy

Advanced injectable biologic medications for moderate-to-severe psoriasis, atopic dermatitis, and hidradenitis suppurativa, with expert monitoring.

Surgical & Procedural

Cryotherapy, cyst and lesion excisions, electrodesiccation & curettage, shave and punch biopsies, and skin cancer surgery.

Light-Based Treatments

Photodynamic therapy (PDT) for actinic keratoses and pre-cancerous lesions; laser hair removal for cosmetic and HS management.

Cosmetic Dermatology

Botox®, dermal fillers (Juvéderm®, Restylane®), VI Peel & PCA chemical peels, and microneedling for collagen and scar treatment.

Hidradenitis Suppurativa

Comprehensive, specialized HS care including medical management, biologic therapy, and the MODES surgical procedure.
