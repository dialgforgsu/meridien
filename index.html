About the Practice

Committed to the health & beauty of your skin

Meridien Dermatology is dedicated to enhancing the health and appearance of your skin through expert care and the latest dermatological advancements. We provide comprehensive, personalized skincare solutions for every patient.

Led by Dr. So Yeon Paek, our practice serves Dallas and the surrounding areas with a focus on quality, compassion, and clinical excellence — from routine dermatology to complex medical conditions and cosmetic care.

What We Treat

Comprehensive dermatology care

Schedule a Visit

General Dermatology

Routine skin exams, rashes, infections, moles, warts, and comprehensive skin health for patients of all ages.

Skin Cancer

Expert detection, diagnosis, and treatment of melanoma, basal cell carcinoma, and squamous cell carcinoma.

Psoriasis & Eczema

Evidence-based treatment for chronic inflammatory conditions including psoriasis and atopic dermatitis.

Hidradenitis Suppurativa

Specialized, compassionate management of HS including biologic therapy and surgical options.

Biologic Therapy

Advanced injectable biologics for moderate-to-severe inflammatory skin diseases, administered with expert oversight.

Dermatologic Surgery

Surgical excisions, lesion removal, and dermatologic procedures performed with precision and care.

Cosmetic Dermatology

Aesthetic treatments to enhance your skin's appearance — anti-aging, rejuvenation, and skin tone procedures.

Clinical Trials

Access to cutting-edge treatments through clinical research led by Dr. Paek as Principal Investigator.
